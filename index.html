Our Opportunities

APPLIED ENTREPRENEURSHIP

The most important factor that remarkably distinct the Global Research MBA program is Applied Entrepreneurship. While the rest of the top B-School Focuses and relies on The Case Method, here at 3G, we not only encourage students to build 1000 crore company from scratch but made it mandatory in their curriculum.

OPPORTUNITIES TO BECOME FOUNDER

2 year Global Research MBA program will consist of 10 business ideas which took 5 years of designing that is strategically designed to create a social impact and 1000Crore turn over. In the two years span, the student based on their performance can be selected to become the Co-Founder of the company assigned to them.

ACCESS YOUR AMERICAN DREAMS

There is an option in 3G Global research MBA to select, which will give the student an opportunity to have a career in America after their 2 year program.

JOB ASSURANCE

3G Global Research MBA offers students job assurance. Our students will be either placed in the company they were assigned to or 3G will assist them placement with our partner companies.
